Our Services

Land Surveying & Sales Services

Boundary Survey

Determine the exact boundaries of a property to avoid disputes and ensure clarity in ownership.

Parcel Identification

Identify and verify individual land parcels, providing essential information for land transactions and legal processes.

Sectional Property

Specialized surveying for sectional or condominium properties, ensuring accurate division and management.

Engineering Survey

Support construction projects with precise surveying data, including elevations, terrain analysis, and alignment details.

Consultations

Professional guidance on all land-related questions, from property purchases to development regulations.

Cadastral Survey

Conduct cadastral surveys to define property boundaries for legal documentation and land registration.

Beacon Replacement

Replace or restore boundary markers (beacons) that have been removed, ensuring property limits are clearly marked.

Topographical Survey

Capture the natural and man-made features of the land to inform design, planning, and development projects.

Mutation Surveys

Adjust property boundaries through mutation surveys to support changes in land ownership and management.
